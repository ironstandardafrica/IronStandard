The Man Behind the Mission

Emmanuel Adakole
Michael (Ra'ah)

Emmanuel Adakole Michael — known as Ra'ah — is a founder, strategist, and apostolic leader whose life's work sits at the intersection of economic sovereignty, technological innovation, and Kingdom mandate. His vision is not confined to sectors. It spans them.

He leads Iron Mint Stewardship Limited and its subsidiaries with the conviction that building well is an act of worship.

What He Builds

Economic Infrastructure

Through TASARA, Ra'ah is designing the coordination and identity infrastructure for Africa's sovereign economic future — starting with the Stewardship Economy and building toward continental scale.

Technology Systems

Through Valerium Systems Nexus, he leads the development of intelligent digital systems, platforms, and infrastructure that serve individuals, institutions, and governments across Africa.

Kingdom Leadership

As Apostle and Prophetic Teacher, Ra'ah carries a distinct apostolic mandate — equipping leaders to operate with Kingdom understanding in every sphere of life, across Africa and the nations.

"The domains of economy, technology, and Kingdom mission are not separate — they are integrated expressions of the same mandate: to build, to steward, and to sustain." — Emmanuel Adakole Michael
