The Assembly

Not a Denomination.
A Mission.

Ekklēsia Omni Missions is not a church in the institutional sense. It is a mission — an apostolic assembly of men and women who have received a mandate to disciple nations, not just congregations.

Led by Ra'ah (Emmanuel Adakole Michael), Ekklēsia operates at the intersection of spiritual leadership, economic reformation, and cultural discipleship — advancing the Kingdom of God in every sphere of life.

It is the spiritual foundation of the entire Iron Mint ecosystem. Every entity — TASARA, Valerium, and Iron Mint itself — operates under the prophetic oversight and Kingdom alignment of Ekklēsia.

"Africa will not be saved by aid but by alignment — alignment with the Creator's design for economies, technologies, and communities." — Ra'ah, Emmanuel Adakole Michael
Our Mandate

Four Dimensions of Kingdom Work

Ekklēsia's mission is expressed across four integrated areas of Kingdom activity.

Apostolic Leadership

Building and releasing apostolic leaders across Africa with the authority to reform institutions, economies, and communities.

Prophetic Teaching

Equipping the body of Christ with Kingdom intelligence through conferences, teaching, and prophetic proclamation across nations.

Nations Discipleship

Discipling entire spheres of society — government, economy, education, media — not merely individuals within those spheres.

Economic Reformation

Bringing Kingdom principles into the marketplace — bridging the spiritual mandate with the economic and technological work of TASARA and Valerium.
