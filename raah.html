Who Is Ra'ah?

Founder, Strategist,
Apostolic Leader

Emmanuel Adakole Michael — widely known as Ra'ah — is a founder, economic strategist, technologist, and apostolic leader whose life's work cannot be confined to a single category. He is the founder and architect of Iron Mint Stewardship Limited and its three operating entities: TASARA, Valerium Systems Nexus, and Ekklēsia Omni Missions.

Ra'ah operates with the conviction that the domains of economy, technology, and Kingdom mission are not separate — they are integrated expressions of the same mandate: to build, to steward, and to sustain. His work across Africa reflects a life oriented around that conviction.

He is based in Liberia and operates extensively across the African continent — advising, teaching, building, and leading at the intersection of the marketplace and the Kingdom.
